Consumer Impact Analysis

When businesses make decisions on overly optimistic data, the end consumer suffers. Here's the chain of consequences.

show_chart

Distorted Markets

Actual customer pain points are underrepresented. The market data paints an inaccurate picture of real consumer sentiment, leading to misallocated resources across the industry.

build_circle

Missed Improvements

The grocery store loses the opportunity to identify and fix actual customer service issues. Problems like cluttered aisles, slow deli service, and parking issues continue unchecked.

sentiment_very_dissatisfied

Eroded Trust

If the real-world experience doesn't match the positive spin, the brand risks reputational backlash. Customers who feel ignored will eventually leave—and tell others.
