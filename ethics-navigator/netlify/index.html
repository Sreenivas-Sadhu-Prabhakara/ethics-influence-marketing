What Happened at
Trend Bridge?

Eleanor Carrington, a newly hired research associate at Trend Bridge Market Research Inc., designed a customer satisfaction survey for a grocery store client using balanced, objective methodology.

Without her knowledge, her carefully crafted questions were secretly rewritten to guarantee positive responses. Every open-ended question was removed. The final survey was a tool of client appeasement, not genuine research.

When she discovered the manipulation during a client presentation, her supervisor's response was clear: "Pleasing the client takes precedence over strict objectivity."
